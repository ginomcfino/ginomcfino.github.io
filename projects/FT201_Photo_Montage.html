Postcards from the City

On the evening of October 5th, 2019, I got off the T (Boston's subway system) at Government Center, and I took some black and white photographs of the things that I saw there that day.

The goal of this photo montage is to portray a distinctive character of this location, which I found to be very quiet, isolated, and dystopian. Hope you enjoy this short montage!
